This Week's Instagram Story

Choose a section from the book 100 Habits of Successful Publicaiton Designers. Paraphrase the entire section as its own Instagram story. Turn every sentence (or phrase, if there are many points made in a given sentence) into its own story panel composed of text and/or visuals. Begin the story with a quick introduction explaining you are presenting one section of the book.
