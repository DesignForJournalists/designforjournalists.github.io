Instastories

We had a class Instagram account, @designforjournalists. Every week, a few students took control of the account, and each was assigned to compose an Instagram Story on an given subject. Subjects ranged from noteworthy magazines to principles of good design. Students were challenged not only to research and relay their topic, but to do so leveraging the possiblities and constraints inherent to the Instagram Story creation and reading experiences. Below are a few class examples.

Left to right: Mollie Gold on Michael Bierut’s Yale poster designs; India Fizer on Rookie; Alyssa Achacoso on “overdesigning.”
