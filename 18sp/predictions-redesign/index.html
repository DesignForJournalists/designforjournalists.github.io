Part 01 Assignment

Systems Design

For Part 01 of the semester, students collaborated in groups to strategically and systematically rethink the redesign of the NiemanLab 2018 Predictions website. Below are write-ups explaining each group's redesigned website, and links to redesigns. Here is the original assigment:

You will work in a group of 3-4 students. Work with your group to build a website using HTML/CSS. The website should have one homepage and 3-4 article pages (depending on the number of people in your group). You will use the content and elements from . By default, there should be content parity. Any subtractions or additions should be a deliberate move to improve the site. Each group will assign each member a specific role (lead designer, lead developer, lead editor, product manager, etc). Each person in the group will be responsible for designing/coding their own article page, and each person's article page should draw use the exact same CSS file. The homepage should be designed/coded together. Finally, you will write a project writeup that outlines what you did, and what problems/strengths/tenets drove your redesign/development

Final note: This webpage itself and the write-up pages were designed and coded using a template based on one of the group's redesign project.
