Journalism + Design: Predictions for 2019 / about

In the spring of 2018, journalism students from The New School partnered with design students from The Rhode Island School of Design. Each team wrote an article identifying an important challenge in the field of journalism. The article had to include a prediction about how the industry will respond to the challenge, and a formal proposal for one idea of a product design solution (a website, app, browser extension, installation, etc.).

The New School students are from the “Design for Journalists II” class in the Journalism+Design program. The RISD students are from the “Editorial Design for Screen” in the graphic design program. I’m Dylan Greif, instructor for both classes.
