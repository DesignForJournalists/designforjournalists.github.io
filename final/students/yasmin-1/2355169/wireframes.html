The "Rouge Rouge Kiss" Experience

May 12, 2016

The opening page when you get to the website.

Set up your name and your partner's name as well as the colo of lipstick you would like to use.Then send a generated URL to your friend through the below buttons, (Facebook Messenger, iMessage, WhatsApp etc.)

The invitation your partner recieves when they open the generated URL sent to them.

The kiss page with countdown allowing your friend to click on choose their lipstick color and join the page.

Some effects such as the background flashing red in the last few seconds before the user kisses the screen.

Kiss the screen!

Both users are required to kiss the screen for three seconds, the kiss of the other user will show up faintly on both users screen.

After three seconds the background of the kisses will become an animation which can be personalized and chosen from a number of options.

The animation continues for several seconds as background expands and more elements are added as time progresses.

The background is cropped as a square and made to be a sharable image through Facebook, Instagram, iMessage etc.
