09-01 Assignment

Hunt for Type

Go outside, armed with a camera

Look for examples of 6 different type classifications: humanist serif, rational serif, slab serif, grotesque sans serif, geometric sans serif, script.

For each, snap a picture of one letter that exemplifies the category well.

Open the Layout app, but Instagram. Use this app to arrange your selections on a grid. Have fun with it. Crop and scale the letters however you like. The entire letter does not have to show in its square, but enough should show of it so that you can identify the type classification.

Submit: Save the image to your phone, and send it directly to the @designforjournalists Instagram account by 10am next Thursday.

Submit: Turn your image into a quiz sheet that you will share with the class. The quiz sheet will ask quiz takers which square reflects which category. It should be made on a letter size paper. It's up to you to decide the copy the worksheet needs and how best to design it for quiz takers. Print out 20 copies and bring it to class.
