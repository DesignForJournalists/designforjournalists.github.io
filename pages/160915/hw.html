This Week's Instagram Story

Both stories this week will profile the typography of an article an online magazine. Choose an article from the magazine assigned to you. Briefly describe what kind of magazine it is, and what this article is about. Then spend most of your story on: what are the type classifications used, what kind of personality do they have, how are different type styles being used functionally (for headers, section labels, body type, etc.).
