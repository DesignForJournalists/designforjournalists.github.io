10-13 Assignment

Midterm Project: The Zine Feature Article

  • Refer to original assignment for details.
  • Meet with your collaborator to...
  • Land on one direction for your zine.
  • Generate 1000 words in a Word/Google doc that explore your zine's feature article topic.
  • Generate three 11x17 pages of visual forms the explore your zine's feature article design.
  • Draw on the words and forms you generated to draft three feature article cover pages, which should include: Title, byline, lede/excerpt.
  • UPLOAD one folder containing pdfs of your writing and forms. Label the one file words.pdf and the other forms.pdf, and label the folder your first names, lowercase, in alphabetical order, separated by a hyphen (ex: arel-shana). Upload to our shared google drive folder by 10:00AM next Thursday.
  • Present your work to a small group. You must present it using both words (writing, spoken) and visuals (sketches, images, examples).
