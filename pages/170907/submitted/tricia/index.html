A Quote by Audre Lorde

Audre Lorde (1934-1992) was a black author, feminist, lesbian, and civil rights activist. Her poetry is known for it's emotional expression and exploration of the black, female identity. Lorde was born in New York City to Caribbean immigrant parents and much of her work is influenced by her upbringing.

"It is not our differences that divide us. It is our inability to recognize, accept, and celebrate those differences."
