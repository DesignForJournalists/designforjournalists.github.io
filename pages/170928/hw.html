Paragraph Design for Print (Cont)

  • Choose an interview from The Great Discontent website. Scrape the text (not the images). You will completely redesign the article as a print layout.
  • The layout should be in spread format, at least 3 pages long, and each pages dimension should be 6.5" wide x 9" high.
  • Incorporate all parts of the articles anatomy including: title, byline, deck, introductory body text, pullquotes, captions. Use the "paragraph styles" panel to style each element.
  • Optional: Use Fonts from Adobe Typekit.
  • You are welcome to add additional text and graphics to suit your editorial and design needs.
  • At the end of the article, include a section with: The name of the assignment, the class name, the instructor name, your name, and the source where you found the original article.
  • EXPORT your pages as a Print PDF. In the dialogue box: under Setup, toggle "Spreads"; under Marks and Bleed, toggle Crop Marks and Bleed).
  • PRINT the pdf on 11"x17" paper and bring it to class.
  • UPLOAD the pdf to our shared google drive folder by 10:00AM next Thursday. Label the pdf your first name (ex: dylan.pdf – one word, all lowercase).
