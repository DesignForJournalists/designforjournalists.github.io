Paragraph Design for Print (Cont)

  • Choose an interview from The Great Discontent website. Scrape the text and images. You will completely redesign the article as a print layout.
  • The layout should be in spread format, at least 3 pages long, and each pages dimension should be 8"w x 10"h.
  • Incorporate all parts of the articles anatomy including: title, byline, deck, introductory body text, pullquotes, captions. Use the "paragraph styles" panel to style each element.
  • Use Fonts from Adobe Typekit.
  • You are welcome to add additional text and graphics to suit your editorial and design needs.
  • At the end of the article, include a section with: The name of the assignment, the class name, the instructor name, your name, and the source where you found the original article.
  • PRINT your pages in spread format on 11" x 17" paper. (Under Setup, toggle Scale to Fit, and under Marks and Bleed, toggle Crop Marks). You do not need to trim your spreads. Bring it to class.
  • EXPORT AS A PDF your work (in Adobe PDF (Print) format). Label the pdf your first name (ex: dylan.pdf – one word, all lowercase).
  • UPLOAD the pdf to our shared google drive folder by 10:00AM next Thursday.
