Searching For Magic In Dark Times
In Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, headmaster Albus Dumbledore explains to the students of Hogwarts the value of staying positive, even when hope seems to be lost.
"Happiness can be found, even in the darkest of times, if one only remembers to turn on the light."
-Albus Dumbledore, written by Steven Kloves for the screenplay of the 2004 film adapation of the third installment of J.K. Rowling's beloved book series
