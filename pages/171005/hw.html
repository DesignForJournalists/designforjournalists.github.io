Zine Project

We will discuss the project details and deadlines next week. For now:

  • Read: this article by Rookie magazine about zines.
  • Think of an audience in New York City you want to make a zine for (your classmates, your local park, people who like to knit...)
  • Think of an exact time and place you want a zine to be distributed (the subway, a dinner party, a rooftop...)
  • Come up with 5 different zine ideas. Brainstorm each idea with words (lists, descirptions, sentence fragments) and images (scribbles, illustrations, storyboards). Densely fill up 5 regular sheets of paper, front and back, with your brainstorm of ideas. Let the brainstorm of words and images help you arrive at your 5 ideas. By the end, each idea should have a specific audience and specific distribution time/place in mind.
  • Bring your sketches into class. You will break up into groups and share your ideas with others.
