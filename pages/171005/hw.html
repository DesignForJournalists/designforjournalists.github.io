Paragraph Design for Print (Cont)

  • Design a polished version of your article spreads.
  • Choose fonts from Adobe Typekit. (The fonts you choose can be based on your web project, or new). Next week, be able to describe the category and quality of the fonts you choose; justify your reason for picking it.
  • Apply a column/grid to your page layout.
  • Every spread of your design must include at least one formal typographic component. (Maybe it's a detail of an enlarged letterform of certain font, maybe it's a pattern of small letterforms used as a background, etc....). The goal is to let the formal qualities of typography inspire interesting and meaningful design compositions.
  • EXPORT AS A PDF your work (in Adobe PDF (Print) format). Label the pdf your first name (ex: dylan.pdf – one word, all lowercase).
  • This time: PRINT your pages (Under Setup, toggle Scale to Fit, and under Marks and Bleed, toggle Crop Marks). Bring it to class.
  • UPLOAD the pdf to our shared google drive folder by 10:00AM next Thursday.
