Zine Project (cont.)

We will discuss the project details and deadlines next week. For now:

  • Choose one of your zine concepts for your project.
  • Design a first draft of your zine. It should have multiple sections. If you don't yet have a very specific idea what the multiple sections will be, then have it include these sections: a/ A letter from the editor, b/ A light 1-2 page "front-matter" article, c/ A 4-5 page feature article, and d/ A one-page light "end-matter" article.
  • The design of your first draft does not have to include real content for each section. Think "impressionistically." Have an idea of what each section should be, and use placeholder text, placeholder images/boxes, and design to give form to what the section will be. (You will follow up with real content after).
  • EXPORT AS A PDF your work (in Adobe PDF (Print) format). Label the pdf "zine-" your first name (ex: zine-dylan.pdf – one word, all lowercase, no spaces).
  • UPLOAD the pdf to our shared google drive folder by 10:00AM next Thursday.
