Typographic Layouts (Assigned 2/11)

Look for five inspiring quotes, each one 700 – 1000 characters long. Design five different 8"w x 10"h layouts. Each layout should be inspired by a different chosen quote. All should be in black and white. Each should use just one typeface (but you may use different weights and styles). Each should include a header, the quote itself, and an attribute. Think about: balance, legibility, dynamism, softness, loudness... Play with: font size, scale, leading, alignments, contrast, margins...

Formatting:

  • Use Typekit to find your fonts, and complete this project in InDesign.
  • Print your pages (Under "Setup", toggle "Scale to Fit", and under "Marks and Bleed" toggle "Crop Marks").
  • Then "Export..." (in "Adobe PDF (Print)" format) your work as a 5-page PDF.
  • Name your PDF your first name, all lowercase (name.pdf). Upload the file to our shared google drive “Assignments” folder by 11:00AM next Thursday.
