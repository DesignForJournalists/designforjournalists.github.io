Article Layouts (Assigned 2/18)

Pick an article on The Great Discontent and use the content (images, header, subtitle info, pull quotes, captions, body copy, etc.). Design this article on a spread of two 8 inch x 10 inch pages. (The original assignment was 8.5 x 11 inches, which is fine, but 8 x 10 will be better for printing.) Your design may run multiple spreads. You will complete ONE variation of the spread. (The original assignment had you doing 3 variations. Now it's one, so try multiple directions, but make one good). Include only the content that is a part of the article (i.e. you do not have to include the website's logo, navigation, or other magazine-level content. Use a grid, and be ready to describe it).

Formatting:

  • Use Typekit to find your fonts, and complete this project in InDesign.
  • Print your pages by clicking Edit > Print.... This will open a dialog box. Then:
    • At the top under Printer, select your printer.
    • If your printer can print on 11" x 17" paper: In the General tab in the siderail, toggle Spreads. Then click the Page Setup button at the bottom to open a new dialog box. Here, select the paper size of Tabloid (a.k.a. 11" x 17") and choose the landscape orientation. Click OK.
    • In the Marks and Bleed tab in the siderail, toggle Crop Marks.
    • Click Print.
  • Export... (in Adobe PDF (Print) format) your work as a PDF.
  • Name your PDF your first name, all lowercase (“name.pdf”). Upload the file to our shared google drive Assignments folder by 11:00AM next Thursday.
