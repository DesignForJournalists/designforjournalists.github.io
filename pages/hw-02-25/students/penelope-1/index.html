On Art

Art has always been my salvation. And my gods are Herman Melville, Emily Dickinson, Mozart.

I believe in them with all my heart. And when Mozart is playing in my room, I am in conjunction with something I can’t explain — I don’t need to.I know that if there’s a purpose for life, it was for me to hear Mozart.Or if I walk in the woods and I see an animal, the purpose of my life was to see that animal.I can recollect it, I can notice it. I’m here to take note of.And that is beyond my ego, beyond anything that belongs to me, an observer, an observer.

Maurice Sendak
