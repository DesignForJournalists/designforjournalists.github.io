Typographic Web Layouts (Assigned 03-03)

Design Assignment

Your 2/18 assignment was to design a print spread of an article from The Great Discontent. Do the same thing, but now, as a web page.

  • Include a header, subheader, bio/byline, lead, questions and answers, captions, pullquotes.
  • You do not need to include images, since we haven't covered this yet. You can just insert a blank gray box instead.
  • Use Google fonts and custom colors.
  • Your page should look excellent on both desktop and mobile. For this assignment, I care more about how you structure the page elements, and less about stunning visual eye candy. Practice and demonstrate your (ever-improving) ability to style and position elements differently and properly on the page.
  • Your layout should include at least one section where: two or more divs with set widths are positioned side by side. (At least, for desktop browsers).
  • Submit your article as a folder with its own html and css file. Label the folder firstname. Label the html file in the folder index.html
  • Upload the folders to our shared google drive Assignments folder by 11:00AM next Thursday.
