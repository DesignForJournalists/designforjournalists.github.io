Article Layouts Cont. (Assigned 03-17)

Where the design of journalism is going...

Write, design, art-direct, and edit an 800-1000 word article that poses one idea about where the design of journalism is going. Draw on two case studies for your analysis. One case study will be assigned to you from Designing the Editorial Experience. The second can be from the book, or elsewhere.

The two case studies can reinforce a single vision of where the design of journalism is going, or it can pose two conflicting views on the same issue.

Include an original quote from someone you know.

Design a simple navigation at the top that links to the following url: designforjournalists.github.io/pages/where-design-journalism-going via the word: Home.

Reference your sources, attribute your graphics, be a good journalist.

Submit: Submit your working folder containing your html and css files. Label the html file index.html and the folder firstname-1. Upload the folder to our shared google drive folder “Assignments” by 11:00AM of our next Thursday class.

First Steps: What Kind of Magazine Would You Make?

For our next class, throw together three ideas for an editorial publication that you would start. What would the subject matter be? Who would be the audience? How often would you publish it? Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

Present 2-4 rough sketches for each of your ideas.

Present 2-4 examples of existing or past editorial publications that you will draw on.

Submit: Make your presentation as a basic web page. Submit it as working folder containing your html and css files. Label the html file index.html and the folder firstname-2. Upload the folder to our shared google drive folder “Assignments” by 11:00AM next Thursday.
