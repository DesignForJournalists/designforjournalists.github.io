Final Proposals

Entertainment Publication

What would the subject matter be?

For this publication I would like to have it be all about the TV/Film/Theater entertainment industry. For this one I have drawn inspiration from Entertainment Weekly, Vulture, and Bustle. I really admire the way in which both Vulture and Bustle analyze and critique TV shows and film, and show a deeper aspect to what we watch than just plain fodder. And that's something that I've always been interested in-mostly because I watch too much TV-and I think it would be interesting to create something like that.

Who would be the audience?

I think the audience for this would be just a general TV/Movie watching audience-with an added theater loving audience. But more specifically I would like for the publication to highlight the lack of diversity within Hollywood, Broadway, and the entertainment industry in general. I want to have a strong presence among women and people of color-and others who don't see themselves as represented in mainstream media.

How often would you publish it?

It would be continous publication since I would plan for it to have a mostly online presence.

Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

It would be a medium-sized (almost blog style) site.

Latinx Teens and Young Adult Publication

What would the subject matter be?

This publication would deal with the ins and outs of being a Latinx teen/young adult. I drew a lot of inspiration from Rookie, which is a very creative and expressive. I want there to be a space that is by these young people who don't necessarily have seen themselves in other forms of media. I also drew inspiration from Remezcla and We Are Mitu. Both sites cover Latino events, culture, music, food, and even news-which I see as something that could also be added contributions to the site.

Who would be the audience?

The intended audience would be young Latinx people.

How often would you publish it?

It would have a continous presence online but I also think (a little Rookie style) that once a month an issues could be published in print with contributions and submissions. Whether it's poetry or photography and even news articles.

Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

I see it as both an independent mag and a website

News Publication

What would the subject matter be?

The subject for this publication would be strictly news. Ranging from news in the U.S. to a style section to tech and politics. I drew on inspiration from Mic, Mashable and a bit from the NYTimes when thinking about this site, because I very much admire the way they design their pages (plus the different and varying sections they have) to make news a lot more approachable and managable for a reader.

Who would be the audience?

The audience would be anyone within any given age-range that is interested in current events. I would like to make it pretty accessible for both millenials and older generations.

How often would you publish it?

It would be on continous publication online.

Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

Large news site
