Idea One: Rapture

  • What would the subject matter be?

    • It would be based on the idea of taking a cigarette break. Since rapture means "a feeling of intense pleasure or joy," the website would be a short, simple and sweet EVERYTHING-YOU-NEED-TO-KNOW about the news, entertainment etc. magazine. The intense joy/pleasure part would come from the idea that it's a smoke break when you'd be reading these articles.

  • Who would be the audience?

    • The audience, I guess, naturally, would be smokers since the idea is during a smoke break, but really anyone who works who takes breaks and is interested in keeping up to day with the latest.

  • How often would you publish it?

    It would be published everyday, generated by things like Twitter, facebook NYT and stuff and bring you the latest news you need to know what's being talked about

  • Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

    • It would be a news site, not necessarily large but more of a generator to collect all the information through analytics that is trending or important etc. It could also have a small mag publication with feature pieces on what was talked about online, sort of like IRE.

    Idea Two: ArtweAll

  • What would the subject matter be?

    • It would be all about Art within the city, sort of like Art forum but with a younger generations perspective and with younger less-wellknown upcoming artists interviewed and listed.

  • Who would be the audience?

    • The audience would be young art lovers and anyone who wants to see what the lives of the upcoming art world looks like.

  • How often would you publish it?

    • It would be a monthly publication.

  • Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

    • It would be a small independent magazine, but would hopefully have a large following (something similar to WAX).

    Idea Three: FEMALE

  • What would the subject matter be?

    • It would be all about female empowerment and the latest news/interviews that have to do with females in the world. It would have categories like Art, Entertainment, etc. and each category would have information or interviews or videos/photos of females in that category. It is a magazine that is trying to empower women by giving the latest news about women doing great things in the world.

  • Who would be the audience?

    • The audience would hopefully be everyone really. But mainly women of the younger generation and also older generations.

  • How often would you publish it?

    • it would be published online weekly, but have a bi-monthly small print magazine that covers the top women that month or top stories.

  • Would it be a small independent mag, a large news site, or something else?

    • It would be both small and large. The magazine would be a small zine-esque but the online magazine would be large and newsy- but also creative.
