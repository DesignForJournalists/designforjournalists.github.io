One World, One Love

Concept: A global online community-run publication based on social justice and intercultural idea exchange. A crosssection between international news, politics, storytelling and change-maker forums.

Content:

  • International News Coverage
  • Independent Opinion Blog Entries
  • Inspiring People Profiles
  • On-This-Day: History Glance
  • Yea or Nay: What's Happening On The Hill
  • Podcast of The Month
  • Change-Maker Tools:

    • -Upcoming Events (ie. Public Lectures, Protests, Rallies, Benefit Concerts, Poetry Slams, Book Events, etc.)
    -Networking
    -List of Organizations To Get Involved With
    -Wise Words (Inspirational, Cheese-Free Quotes)
    -Job Opportunities
    -Reader Mail

    Header: Large, page-filling illustration or graphic of the globe that is updated daily with reader-submitted art, i.e. think Google. Title sits at bottom right corner of header image.

    Dashboard: Mix of medias displayed: videos, audio, photos, and breaking news headlines. Drop-down menu with all content categories located just under header. Inspiring people profiles are at the bottom of the home page and scroll horizontally. Yea or Nay, On-This-Day, and Change-Maker Tool kit links are all represented by illustrations that link to a seperate page.

    Footer: Continuation of header image.
