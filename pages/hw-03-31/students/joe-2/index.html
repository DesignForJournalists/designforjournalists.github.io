Sketch 1

Motivation Magazine

We Give You Good Excuses

This would be an annual publication that would showcase motivational stories, people, claimed miracles, and situations that went in a complete 360. This annual publication would only be availble in print. It would be targetted to every demographic and would have the following sections:

Finance
School
Work
Home Life
Spirituality
Success stories
Overcoming the odds
Miracles?

The idea behind this is to target people who are otherwise at the end of their rope or somewhat lost in their lives.It would be in print because I want it to reach everyone from the homeless to people sitting in doctor's offices. It would be published annually due in part to the time it would take gathering real quality content. This publication would be run independently.

Inspiration for this site: Vibe Magazine.

image

I was inspired by Vibe Magazine for this because of its raw interviews, artistic nature and appeal in range of economic class.

Sketch 1

Closed Eye Witness

The News In An Alternate Universe

This would be a weekly publication that would present the news as if it had good outcomes rather than the grim truths the media serves on a daily basis. So for example, in the publication, terrorist attacks would have never happened. Instead the attacks would have been thwarted by a gang of kittens or something along those lines. The Publication would cater to everyone as a audience, rely heavily on humor and would be availble in online format and print. Some of its sections would be:

Would News
What Unicorns Did This Week In Washington
Work
Subway Leprechauns
Medical Breakthroughs
Prophet's Corner
Miracles?

Inspiration for this site:Mad Magazine.

image

I was inspired by Mad Magazine for this publication because as a kid I remember how this magazine made light of very serious topical events and set itself apart almost creating its own world on paper.
