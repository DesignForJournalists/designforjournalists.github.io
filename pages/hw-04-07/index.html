What kind of magazine would you make? (cont.)

Choose one of your three ideas for an editorial publication that you would found

Whatever the publication you conceive, post a Letter from the Editor as a “sneak peak” article that introduces your publication to your new audience.

In article, clearly explain:

  • What your audience is. Address them.
  • What the subject matter will be.
  • Why this publication is important to your audience right now.
  • How often you will publish it, and how your audience can access it

Include 2-4 wireframes and/or mockups that show the end-to-end flow of your magazine, and the important views/pages that demonstrate its value.

Make sure you style the post in a way that reinforces your publication’s brand identity.

Submit: Submit it as working folder containing your html and css files. Label the html file index.html and the folder firstname-1. Upload the folder to our shared google drive folder “Assignments” by 11:00AM next Thursday.
