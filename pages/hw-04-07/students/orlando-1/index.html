Welcome to The Photo Chronicles!

by Orlando Mendiola

April 14, 2016

Its 2016 and photography has taken on a whole new meaning. With the abundance of social media and smartphones, cameras are more portable and easier to access than ever. “The Photo Chronicles,” is a site dedicated to all things photography. You will find tips on how to take better smartphone pictures. Whether you use an Android or iPhone or any other type of smartphone, you will find daily tips and blogs about how to improve your photography. If Instagram and iPhone cameras aren’t your thing, don’t worry! You will also find the latest news and reviews on the latest and greatest digital and film cameras. With daily posts, this will be your one-stop shop on everything about photography! Here’s a sneak peek on what to expect!
