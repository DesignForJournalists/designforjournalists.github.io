Intro:

Over the course of the semester, Design for Journalists I had for purpose to teach and improve my knowledge and skills in elements of visual and editorial design such as type classifications, visual hierarchy and layouts, within the context of journalism. To practice the theory learned in both course reading materials and class lectures, I completed assignments such as identifying type classifications in the real world, designing paragraph layouts for both digital and print formats, and producing the first issue of a zine.

This website serves as a portfolio, presenting all the assignments completed this semester along with personal reflections.
