Overview

  • You will write and design a reflective document for your work in this class.
  • The reflective document should:
    - a document title
    - a byline (plus a title and graduation year)
    - the date
    - a dek that explains this course and purpose of the document
    - A section for each project: 1) Hunt for Type; 2) Paragraph Design for Web; 3) Paragraph Design for Print; 4) Zine project
    - Each project section should include: 1) title; 2) assignment description; 3) 1-2 images of your work; 4) a description of your work. Note: Each description should explain your decisions behind at least 3 of the following areas: audience; distribution, color palette; fonts; typographic hierarchy; grid/spacing; graphics/imagery; collaboration. Try to cover as many as you can across all sections.
  • A final section that highlights at least one important value, process, tool, or technique that came uip in class which you will bring into your future student and/or professional work.
