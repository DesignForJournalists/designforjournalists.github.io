Zine Project – Final Work



Below is the work students submitted for the midterm. (You can view the full assignment description here.) Each student conceived, authored, designed, printed, and produced the very first issue of a zine. Their starting point was defining a specific distribution method for a specific audience in a specific time and place, and determining the content and concept from there. Through subject matter, voice, tone, brand, and means of distribution, the final product needed to evoke a certain tone, personality, set of values, and perspective. It could be any size, but had to be bound in a single signature, printed in black and white ink, and include: a cover, a masthead, an editor’s letter, a front-matter article, a feature article, and a back-matter article. Three copies needed to be produced.
